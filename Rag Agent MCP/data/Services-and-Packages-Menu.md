# Services & Packages Menu
### Home Renovation & Interior Design

Transforming spaces, one detail at a time. Whether you need a simple refresh or a complete architectural overhaul, we have a package built for your vision, your timeline, and your budget.

---

## 🎨 Tier 1: Interior Styling & Consultation
**"The Refresh"**

**Best for:** Homeowners and renters who love their layout but want their space to look and feel brand new — without construction, permits, or downtime. Perfect for a single room refresh, a rental upgrade, or anyone testing the waters with professional design.

**What's Included:**
- 1-on-1 design consultation (in-home or virtual)
- Custom mood board & style direction
- Full color palette and paint consultation
- Furniture and decor sourcing list (curated to your budget)
- Layout and furniture placement guidance
- Lighting and accessory recommendations
- Shopping concierge service (we find it, you approve it)
- One round of revisions on the design concept

**What's NOT Included:** Structural changes, contractor coordination, custom-built furniture, on-site construction supervision.

**Timeline:** Typically 1–3 weeks from consultation to final mood board.

**Pricing:** *Starting from $X / per room* — *Custom Quote available for multi-room styling*

## 🛠️ Tier 2: Single-Room Transformation
**"The Signature Space"**

**Best for:** Homeowners ready to fully reinvent one high-impact room — a dream kitchen, a spa-like master bathroom, or a show-stopping living room. Ideal for anyone who wants real design *and* real renovation, with a defined scope and a defined space.

**What's Included:**
- Everything in Tier 1, plus:
- In-depth space planning and minor spatial remodeling (non-load-bearing walls, layout optimization)
- 3D renderings and digital walkthroughs of your new space
- Material, fixture, and finish selection (countertops, tile, flooring, hardware)
- Custom or semi-custom cabinetry design (kitchen/bath tiers)
- Contractor and trades coordination for the selected room
- On-site supervision during renovation
- Procurement and delivery management for materials and furnishings
- Final styling and staging reveal

**What's NOT Included:** Multi-room renovation, full architectural restructuring, whole-home project management.

**Timeline:** Typically 6–12 weeks depending on scope (design + build).

**Pricing:** *Starting from $X / room (Kitchen, Bathroom, or Living Room)* — *Custom Quote based on square footage and finish level*

## 🏛️ Tier 3: Full-Home Design & Turnkey Renovation
**"The Complete Transformation"**

**Best for:** Homeowners undertaking a whole-home renovation, new build interior, or major remodel who want a single expert team managing every decision — from architectural layout to the final throw pillow. This is our most comprehensive, white-glove offering.

**What's Included:**
- Everything in Tier 2, plus:
- Comprehensive top-to-bottom spatial restructuring and floor plan redesign
- Full architectural and structural design coordination
- Custom cabinetry and millwork throughout the home
- Architectural lighting design and electrical planning
- Whole-home material, finish, and fixture palette (cohesive design language across every room)
- Dedicated project manager overseeing contractors, trades, and timelines
- Full permitting and code compliance coordination
- Weekly progress reports and milestone walkthroughs
- Site supervision from demolition to final punch list
- Full-service procurement (furniture, art, textiles, accessories)
- White-glove installation and final professional staging
- Post-renovation walkthrough and touch-up service

**What's NOT Included:** N/A — this is our end-to-end, turnkey solution.

**Timeline:** Typically 4–9 months depending on home size and scope.

**Pricing:** *Starting from $X / project* — *Custom Quote based on square footage, structural complexity, and finish level*
